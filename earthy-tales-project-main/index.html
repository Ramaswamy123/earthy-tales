FAQs

What is stone-ground flour?

Stone-ground flour is a type of flour that is made by grinding whole grains using a traditional stone mill. The millstones used in the process are made of natural stone, such as granite or quartz, which crushes the grains and creates a coarser texture than that of modern roller mills.
